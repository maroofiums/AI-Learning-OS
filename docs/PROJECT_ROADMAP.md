# Success Criteria

A successful AI Learning OS should:

* Be my primary learning platform.
* Organize all notes and projects.
* Understand my knowledge base through AI.
* Help me revise effectively.
* Improve as I learn new technologies.
* Remain modular and maintainable.
* Continue growing for many years without major redesign.
